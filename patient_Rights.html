description Patient Rights

Patient's Bill of Rights and Responsibilities
Patient's has the right to:
  1. Be informed about their rights and responsibilities.
  2. Be informed of the services provided in the hospital.
  3. Be informed of the services provided in the hospital.
  4. Know the identities of the physicians and the other practitioners responsible for his/ her care.
  5. Be treated with respect and dignity at all times and under all circumstances.
  6. Receive care that is respectful of their values and beliefs (provided that they don’t conflict with Islamic rules).
  7. Respect and maintain their privacy during care and according to their needs.
  8. Have a significant other. Chaperon or a staff member from the same gender be present during physical examination, treatment or procedure.
  9. Maintain confidentiality of all information, communication and records related to the care.
  10. Be protected from physical and verbal assault.
  11. Appropriate assessment and management of pain.
  12. Participate in their care including making decisions or asking questions about care and seeking a second opinion without fear or compromising care.
  13. Be educated about their conditions.
  14. Consent to surgery, high risk treatments and procedures anesthesia, and use of blood and blood products, after receiving adequate information.
  15. Refuse or discontinue treatment and be informed of the consequences if the decision, responsibilities related to such decision and available care and treatment alternatives.
  16. Respectful and compassionate care at the end of life.
  17. Be fully informed about clinical research which they agree to participate in, and the right to refuse or withdraw consent without affecting the care provided.
  18. Be informed about donation and transplantation of organs and other tissues as per hospital policy.
  19. Voice a complaint about care or hospital services and have the complaint investigated and resolved whenever is possible.
  20. Have their possessions protected once the hospital assumes responsibilities for protecting possessions or when the patient cannot resume responsibility.
  21. Receive a report on their medical condition according to hospital regulations.
